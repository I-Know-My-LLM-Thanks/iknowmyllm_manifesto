# Core Values

Use the list below to power the "Core Values" section on the site.
Each entry must follow this format:
- Left Value | Right Value | Description

- Evidence | Promises | Verifiable credentials, named references, and demonstrated capabilities over marketing claims or future-oriented projections.
- Transparency | Hype | Clear explanations of limitations and data practices over buzzwords, invented jargon, or claims of proprietary magic.
- Accountability | Obscurity | Named leadership with verifiable histories over anonymous teams, offshore entities, or untraceable ownership.
- Due Diligence | Blind Trust | Systematic verification through independent research over convenience or urgency-driven decisions.
- Human Oversight | AI Worship | Meaningful human control and the ability to understand outcomes over fully automated "hands-off" solutions.
- Community Wisdom | Isolated Judgment | Collective verification and shared experiences over individual assessment or single sources.
